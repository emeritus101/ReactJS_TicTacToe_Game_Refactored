Play Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a very popular game, a paper-and-pencil game, in which 2 players (one using X and the other with O letters try to get first at crossing 3 letters on any diagonal of a 9-cell squared figure.)

Like other paper-and-pencil games, such as Hangman, Dots and Boxes, Sim, The Paper Telephone, Planes, Battleship, it is a mind and thinking games which chalenges the players to outpass their competitors using the power of their mind.

I hope you enjoy it as much as I do.
